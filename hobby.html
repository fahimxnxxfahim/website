About My Hobby

My greatest passion is that of riding my bike with my motor club since I feel free and happy as well as connected with others and the only hobby I like. Whenever we meet to have a ride, I always experience the excitement of ride and a powerful connection amongst us. It does not matter whether we want to check the new places out, take a scenic route, or participate in charity events, it will be remembered. Riding is not the only experience of belonging to the club as part of friendship, new adventures, and stress-free life. My motor club is not only a hobby to me but an important aspect of my life.
